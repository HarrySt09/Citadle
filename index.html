How to play

The idea

A secret city is hidden somewhere in the world. Each round, you're shown a different city and told which compass direction you'd travel from the secret city to reach this new one. Use the arrows on the compass and the clue list to narrow it down - then guess!

You have up to 8 clues. Wrong guesses also become clue cities themselves, giving you extra information at the cost of a clue slot.

Reading the clues

Each chip reads: "Tokyo is NE (47°) of the secret city." That means if you stood in the secret city and walked north-east, you'd eventually reach Tokyo. The degree value is the precise bearing.

Arrows on the compass dial radiate from the centre - the centre represents the secret city, and each arrow points toward one of the clue cities from it.
